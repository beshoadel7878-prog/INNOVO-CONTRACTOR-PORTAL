Access & Verification

Follow these steps to enter the site smoothly.

Access & Verification Steps

  • Arrive at the main gate and show your company ID.
  • Sign in and get your visitor or subcontractor badge.
  • Show proof of induction and required training.
  • Confirm your work area and supervisor contact.
  • Collect a site map and emergency info before entry.

Site Arrival

  • Use the main gate and follow security instructions.
  • Park only in assigned subcontractor areas.
  • Keep your ID ready for quick check-in.

Verification

  • Confirm you have the right PPE.
  • Show required permits and task approvals.
  • Receive a badge before entering work zones.

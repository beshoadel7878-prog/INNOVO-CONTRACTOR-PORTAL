Payment Claims

How to submit your claims correctly and on time.

Steps

  • Prepare the claim for the completed scope.
  • Attach required documents and approvals.
  • Submit to the system before the deadline.
  • Track approval status and respond to queries.

Required Documents

  • Signed work completion / inspection evidence.
  • Approved BOQ or variation (if applicable).
  • Timesheets or delivery notes.
  • Supervisor approval email or signature.

Deadline

  • Submit weekly claims every Thursday.
  • Late submissions move to next cycle.
  • Clarifications must be answered within 48 hours.

Quality Requirements

How to deliver work without rejection.

Inspection Process

  • Submit inspection request before completion.
  • Prepare work area and drawings.
  • Attend inspection and close comments.

Checklists

  • Use the approved checklist per trade.
  • Self-check before calling inspection.
  • Attach checklist to inspection request.

Snag Rules

  • Fix snags within agreed time frame.
  • Re-inspection required after fix.
  • Close snags before next activity.

Material Approval

  • Submit material submittals early.
  • No installation before approval.
  • Keep approvals on site.

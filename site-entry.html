Site Entry

Quick essentials before you enter the site.

Safety Rules

  • Sign in and out every day.
  • Stay in your assigned work zone.
  • Follow traffic and lift exclusion zones.
  • Keep walkways clear and clean up daily.

PPE Required

  • Hard hat, safety boots, and high-vis vest.
  • Safety glasses and gloves for your task.
  • Hearing protection in noisy areas.
  • Respirator if dust or fumes are present.

Working Hours

  • Check in at the gate before your shift.
  • Follow site start and finish times.
  • No work outside approved hours.
  • Overtime must be approved.

Emergency Numbers

  • Site supervisor: +00 000 000 000
  • Security desk: +00 000 000 000
  • Safety officer: +00 000 000 000
  • Emergency services: 999 / 911

Digital Induction

Read these points before starting work.

Site Rules

  • Sign in and out every day.
  • Stay in your assigned work zone.
  • Follow traffic and lift exclusion zones.
  • Keep walkways clear and clean up daily.

Safety Requirements

  • Use tools and equipment only if trained.
  • Report hazards and near misses right away.
  • Use fall protection when working at height.
  • Stop work if conditions feel unsafe.

PPE

  • Hard hat, safety boots, and high-vis vest.
  • Safety glasses and gloves for your task.
  • Hearing protection in noisy areas.
  • Respirator if dust or fumes are present.

Do's & Don'ts

  • Do follow supervisor instructions.
  • Do keep your work area tidy.
  • Don't bypass guards or safety devices.
  • Don't use phones while walking on site.

Emergency Instructions

  • Stop work and follow alarms or calls.
  • Go to the nearest safe exit route.
  • Report to the assembly point.
  • Call the site supervisor for help.

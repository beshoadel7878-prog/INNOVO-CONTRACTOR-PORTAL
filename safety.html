Safety

Keep everyone safe on site.

Safety Procedures

  • Use tools only if trained and authorized.
  • Follow traffic and exclusion zones.
  • Stop work if conditions are unsafe.

Required PPE

  • Hard hat, safety boots, and high-visibility vest.
  • Gloves and eye protection based on task.
  • Hearing protection in high-noise areas.

Incident Reporting

  • Report incidents immediately to the supervisor.
  • Complete the incident report form.
  • Share photos if available.

Toolbox Talks

  • Attend toolbox talks before work starts.
  • Ask questions if anything is unclear.
  • Sign attendance after the briefing.

Do & Don't

  • Do keep work areas tidy.
  • Do follow supervisor instructions.
  • Don't bypass safety devices.
  • Don't use phones while walking.
